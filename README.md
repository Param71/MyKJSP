# MyKJSP

> **Empowering minds, shaping futures** — the all-in-one mobile companion for K.J. Somaiya Polytechnic students and faculty.

MyKJSP is a full-stack Android application built for the K.J. Somaiya Polytechnic community. It brings together academics, communication, and campus life into a single, easy-to-use app.

## ✨ Features

### 🎓 Academics
- **Results** — View your semester results at a glance
- **Attendance** — Track your attendance record
- **Timetable** — Access your class schedule
- **Calendar** — Stay on top of important academic dates

### 📢 Communication
- **Chats** — Private messaging between students and faculty
- **Noticeboard** — Instant announcements from the institution

### 🗂️ Resources
- **Library** — Browse and access library resources
- **To-Do** — Personal task manager to stay organized

### 🗺️ Campus
- **Map** — Interactive campus map

### 👤 Profile & Account
- **Personal Profiles** — Full profile with photo, name, enrollment number, email, phone, date of birth, and year of study
- **Somaiya Email Verification** — Verify your `@somaiya.edu` email address for full access
- **Reset Password** — Self-service password reset

### 🎨 Customisation
- **Light / Dark Mode** — Choose the theme that suits you best
